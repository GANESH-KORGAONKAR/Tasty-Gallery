Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Punjabi dish made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a flavorful tomato-onion and cashew-based curry. It is a perfect accompaniment to naan or roti.

Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Course: Curries

Cuisine: Punjabi

Servings: 5

Calories: 288 kcal

Ingredients

For Masala Paste:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 3 tomatoes, sliced
  • 15 cashews

For Curry:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 chilli
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ¾ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 16 cubes paneer
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Instructions

How to Make Gravy Base:

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter in a large kadai.
  2. Add 5 cloves and 2 pods cardamom, and sauté on low flame until aromatic.
  3. Add 2 sliced onions, 2 cloves garlic, and 1 inch ginger. Sauté until onions shrink slightly.
  4. Add 3 sliced tomatoes and 15 cashews. Sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Cover and boil for 10 minutes or until the tomatoes soften.
  6. Cool completely, transfer to a blender, and blend into a smooth paste.
  7. Pass the curry through a filter to remove seeds and skin. Set aside the smooth tomato-onion paste.

How to Make Paneer Butter Masala:

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter in a large kadai. Add 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli, and sauté until aromatic.
  2. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  3. On low flame, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¾ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala, and 1 tsp salt. Sauté until aromatic.
  4. Add the prepared onion-tomato paste and mix well. Cook until the oil separates from the masala paste.
  5. Add 1 cup water and mix well, adjusting the consistency.
  6. Add 2 tbsp cream and mix well. Add 16 cubes of paneer and mix gently. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Finish with 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander. Mix well.

Notes

  • Adjust the level of cream and butter to taste for a richer curry.
  • Kasuri methi adds a unique aroma; do not skip this step.
